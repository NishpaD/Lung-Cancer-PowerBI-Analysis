# Lung Cancer Risk Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Description
This project analyzes lung cancer risk factors using a dataset of **1,000 patient records with 26 clinical and lifestyle variables**. The objective is to identify key contributors to lung cancer severity (Low, Medium, High) and present insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.


## Objectives
- Analyze the impact of lifestyle, environmental, and genetic factors on lung cancer severity  
- Identify high-risk patient segments  
- Enable interactive, multi-dimensional data exploration  

## Dataset
- **Records:** 1,000 patients  
- **Features:** 26 variables  
- **Target Variable:** Cancer Level (Low, Medium, High)  

**Key Variables Include:**
- Smoking, Passive Smoking  
- Air Pollution  
- Alcohol Consumption  
- Genetic Risk  
- Chronic Lung Disease  
- Symptoms (Chest Pain, Fatigue, Coughing Blood, etc.)  


## Features
- Interactive filters for **age, gender, and cancer severity**  
- Comparative analysis of **smoking vs passive smoking**  
- Visualization of **genetic risk distribution across age groups**  
- Multi-factor analysis of **environmental and lifestyle risks**  


## Key Insights
- Smoking and passive smoking are strongly associated with higher cancer severity  
- Air pollution and chronic lung disease significantly increase risk levels  
- Genetic risk varies across different age groups  
- High-risk patient segments can be identified through combined factor analysis  


## Tools & Skills
- Power BI  
- Data Visualization  
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
- Data Cleaning  
- Data Modeling  


## Outcome
Developed an interactive dashboard enabling **data-driven analysis of lung cancer risk factors**, supporting better understanding of disease patterns and risk segmentation.
